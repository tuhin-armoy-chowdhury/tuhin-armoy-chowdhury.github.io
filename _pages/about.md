I am a Software Engineer at [Golden Harvest Infotech Limited](https://ghitbd.com/), where I build scalable backend microservices, AI-integrated healthcare systems, and hardware–software imaging pipelines. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from [Sylhet Engineering College](https://www.sust.edu/144/schools-departments/105/affiliates/17), affiliated with [Shahjalal University of Science & Technology](https://www.sust.edu/). Previously, I worked as a Software Engineer Intern at [Rokkhi IT Limited](https://www.rokkhi.com/).

My research focuses on the foundations and applications of intelligent software systems. I am especially interested in **AI for software engineering**, **agentic AI**, and **trustworthy AI** — studying how autonomous agents, LLM-assisted development, and rigorous security and reliability practices can work together in deployed systems. Related areas I continue to explore include federated learning, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and healthcare data security. My ongoing work includes ActiFed (active edge orchestration in federated learning) and a survey on retrieval-augmented federated learning.

I am currently seeking PhD positions in computer science and AI-related fields starting **Fall 2027**. My research interests include AI for software engineering, agentic AI, and trustworthy AI. I welcome opportunities to collaborate on research at the intersection of intelligent systems, secure distributed learning, and production-grade software engineering.
