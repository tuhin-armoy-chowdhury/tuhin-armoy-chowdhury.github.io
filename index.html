Hi! I am a Software Engineer at Golden Harvest Infotech Limited, working on scalable backend microservices, AI-integrated healthcare systems, and hardware-software imaging pipelines. Previously, I was a Software Engineer Intern at Rokkhi IT Limited. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Sylhet Engineering College (affiliated with Shahjalal University of Science & Technology).

Research

My work focuses on the foundations and applications of intelligent software systems. I enjoy building backend architectures that are reliable at scale, and exploring how AI — especially computer vision, RAG, and agentic workflows — can solve real problems in healthcare, automation, and industry.

My interests span federated learning, agentic AI, healthcare data security, computer vision, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and human-computer interaction (HCI). I try to bring together strong engineering fundamentals with research-driven ideas, whether that means deploying microservices for clinical systems, building CNN pipelines for image classification, or integrating LLM-based automation into production workflows.
